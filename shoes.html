These are the ballet flats in patent mint leather from @idee.fixe
The thing I love about these shoes is that they are really simple but still make a statement.

They make the outfit fancier but are the most comfortable shoes to walk in. The heel is small, kind of muted but it definitely makes the difference. That tiny bit of height (1.5 cm) shows the best features of the leg while boosting your confidence.
